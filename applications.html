PC It Together

Open Application Go to Repo

My first application allows users to create an account, where they can select computer parts to build a desired computer.

PC It Together will show a warning sign if a pair of parts will not be compatible to notify users of potential issues.

Another feature is the ability to share with other users, when creating or editing a build, users have the option to enable sharing to other users.

Technologies: JavaScript, AngularJS, HTML5, CSS3, Node.js, Angular Material, MongoDB, FileStack API, SweetAlerts 2 API.

The Belwin Citizen Science application improves the teaching ability for Belwin Outdoor Science Observancy and increases engagment of visiting students. Students have the ability to use Belwin Citizen Science outdoors without Wi-Fi, and may restore observations if application goes offline. Teachers will be able to present submissions by students to view answers and notes.

Technologies: JavaScript, AngularJS, HTML5, CSS3, Node.js, Angular Material, SQL, Material Design Lite.

Developed with a group of five Full Stack Developer students at Prime Digital Academy.
