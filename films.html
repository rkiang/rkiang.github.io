Following (1998)>

Runtime: 69 mintues

MPAA Rating: R

Genre: Crime, Mystery, Thriller

First feature film directed by Christopher Nolan, about a young man whom follows in the footsteps of a seasoned thief. The low budget prompted the movie to be shot and released in black and white presentation, and to be filmed on weekends when cast and crew were available.

Memento (2000)>

Runtime: 113 mintues

MPAA Rating: R

Genre: Mystery, Thiller

After the murder of his wife and the brain injury that disable him from keeping any new memories after a short time. To avenge his wife's death, Leonard goes through many obsticles from seeking aid from strangers with their own objective to tatooting clues to his body to regain this new memories. This film is presented with the most recent events shown first and backtracking the oldest events.

Insomnia (2002)>

Runtime: 118 minutes

MPAA Rating: R

Genre: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

This is only directorial feature which Nolan was not involved in the writing. The story follows a Los Angeles homicide dectective that is brought to Alaska to investigate a murder of a teenager, and the detective portrayed by Al Pacino, is being investigated by internal affairs and begins suffering from insomnia from his guilt and daylight throughout his stay in Alaska.

Batman Begins (2005)>

Runtime: 140 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Action, Adventure

The first of three Batman films by Nolan focuses on the origins of Bruce Wayne to his journey to become the anti-hero of Gotham City. This would become Nolan's first Hollywood blockbuster and lead to many more ambitious big budget projects to follow.
