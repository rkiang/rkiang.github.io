Following (1998)

Genre: Crime, Mystery, Thriller

First feature film directed by Christopher Nolan, about a young man whom follows in the footsteps of a seasoned thief. The low budget prompted the movie to be shot and released in black and white presentation, and to be filmed on weekends when cast and crew were available.

Batman Begins (2005)

Runtime: 140 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Action, Adventure

The first of three Batman films by Nolan focuses on the origins of Bruce Wayne to his journey to become the anti-hero of Gotham City. This would become Nolan's first Hollywood blockbuster and lead to many more ambitious big budget projects to follow.

The Prestige (2006)

Runtime: 130 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Drama, Mystery, Sci-Fi

19th Century stage magicians become dangerous rivals through a series of sabotaging, and upstaging. The story is presented through many perspectives that reveal the truth behind the mystery.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Runtime: 152 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

The sequeal to Batman Begins (2005) has the Caped Crusader facing off against the Joker, Harvey Dent and the criminal underworld. The battles end up testing the will of Bruce Wayne and his closest allies as the soul of the city is on the line.

Inception (2010)

Runtime: 148 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Through the use of a shared dream technology, skilled thieves take on a impossible task of creating an idea of a rival industrial leader. The film is presented with many layers of dreams coinciding to pull of the job, with each dream going deeper than the last.

The Dark Knight Rises (2012)

Runtime: 164 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Action, Thriller

The final feature in Nolan's Dark Knight Trilogy, after the events of the Dark Knight (2008), Gotham is overtaken by Batman's foes. Batman is set against the intimidating Bane, as Bruce Wayne begins to his process to move on from being Gotham's Dark Knight.

Interstellar (2014)

Runtime: 169 mintues

MPAA Rating: PG-13

Genre: Adventure, Drama, Sci-Fi

With Earth's environment becoming harsher to live, a NASA team must explore through wormholes to discover suitable planets for humanity to call a new home. This space epic has the character's dealing with obstacles from their loved ones on Earth and the uncharted territories that must be reached.
